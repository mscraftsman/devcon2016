Sponsors and Partners
Is your business activity related to IT?

Do you want to participate in the making of the Developers Conference? Great idea!
You will contribute to the success of an event that is unique in Mauritius or even in the Indian Ocean and will receive a remarkable visibility.

Sponsoring this event would be a means of communication for your company: to promote your products and solutions to an audience of developers while getting visibility to regional level.

Members of the MSCC will be present with a stand for informations and registrations. For stand we mean a space at your disposal with 1 or 2 tables, a couple of chairs, network connectivity and power supply.

A folder with the program of the conference and a flyer with sponsors' logos will be given to every participant. it will be possible to expose in all rooms an advertising billboard (a3 format).

Sponsorship options

You can sponsor the Developers Conference in several ways: you can fund one of the many social moments or you can grab one of our sponsor packages.

Social events

During the conference there are various moments for social aggregation between participants: coffee breaks, lunch, social night and pre-dinner aperitifs. Please get in touch with us to arrange the options that best suits your needs, or to share a brilliant sponsoring idea.
